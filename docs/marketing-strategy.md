**2026–2027 | Patriotic Industrial Muscle Brand**

## 1. Target Audience Profiles

### The Weekend Warrior (Primary)
- **Who:** 30–50 year old truck or Jeep owner, owns their vehicle 5+ years
- **Pain:** Overpays at the dealership, can't trust generic parts, wastes time on wrong fitment
- **Motivation:** Save money, do it right, trust the part
- **Where they hang:** YouTube (car channels), Reddit (r/Trucks, r/Jeep), Facebook Groups, Instagram

### The Performance Builder (Secondary)
- **Who:** 22–40 year old building or modifying a muscle car, truck, or Jeep
- **Pain:** Finding brand-name performance parts without fake listings
- **Motivation:** Power, reliability, brand credibility
- **Where they hang:** Instagram, YouTube, car shows, forums (JEEPFORUM, F150Forum)

### The Small Shop Owner (Tertiary)
- **Who:** Independent mechanic, 2–8 bays, mostly domestic vehicles
- **Pain:** Dealer price gouging, slow delivery from existing suppliers
- **Motivation:** Reliable parts fast, shop pricing
- **Where they hang:** LinkedIn (sometimes), trade expos, word of mouth

## 2. Marketing Channels

### Channel 1: Organic Search (SEO)
**Priority:** HIGH | **Timeline:** 0–18 months (compound effect)

**Content Strategy:**
- 2 blog posts/week targeting high-intent keywords
- Keyword clusters: `[make] [model] [year] [part]` (e.g., "2019 Ford F-150 5.0 intake manifold")
- Fitment guides and installation tutorials
- Comparison articles: OEM vs aftermarket, brand vs brand
- Category landing pages optimized per vehicle make

**Target Keywords (Year 1):**
| Keyword | Monthly Searches | Difficulty |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| ford f150 performance parts | 12,000 | Medium |
| jeep wrangler lift kit | 22,000 | High |
| chevrolet silverado parts | 8,400 | Medium |
| gmc sierra exhaust upgrade | 3,200 | Low |
| dodge ram brake pads | 5,500 | Low |
| best cold air intake f150 | 6,800 | Medium |

**Year 1 SEO Goal:** 15,000 organic sessions/month by Month 12

---

### Channel 2: Google Shopping & PPC
**Priority:** HIGH | **Budget:** $3,500/month (starting Month 2)

**Campaigns:**
1. **Google Shopping** — Feed all 200+ SKUs, optimize for high-ticket items
2. **Search Ads** — Brand + vehicle-specific queries
3. **Remarketing** — Cart abandoners (3-day window, 5-touch sequence)
4. **Performance Max** — AI-driven cross-channel (test Month 4)

**KPIs:**
- Target ROAS: 4.5x
- Max CPC on key SKUs: $1.80
- Shopping CTR target: 1.2%

---

### Channel 3: Social Media
**Priority:** MEDIUM | **Budget:** $800/month organic + $1,200/month paid

#### YouTube (Long-Form)
- 2 videos/month: installs, comparisons, vehicle builds
- Format: "How to Replace X on [Year] [Make] [Model]"
- Vehicle Finder tool walkthroughs
- Shop owner testimonials

#### Instagram
- Daily posts: product shots, build reveals, customer submissions
- Reels: 30-second install clips, before/after transforms
- Stories: limited-time deals, polls, Q&A with mechanics

#### Facebook
- Targeted ads: 28–52, male, owns Ford/Chevy/GMC/Jeep/Dodge
- Join and contribute to vehicle-specific groups (non-promotional)
- Facebook Shop integration for seamless purchase

#### TikTok (Year 2)
- Mechanic humor, "satisfying install" videos, truck build series

---

### Channel 4: Email Marketing
**Priority:** HIGH | **Tool:** Klaviyo

**Flows (Automated):**
| Flow | Trigger | Goal |
|------|---------|------|
| Welcome Series (5 emails) | Account signup | 2nd purchase within 30 days |
| Cart Abandonment (3 emails) | Cart, no purchase | Recover 12–18% of abandoned carts |
| Browse Abandonment (2 emails) | Product view, no add to cart | Retarget with social proof |
| Post-Purchase (3 emails) | Order shipped | Review request + cross-sell |
| Win-Back (3 emails) | 90 days inactive | Re-engage with offer |
| VIP Flow | 3+ orders | Exclusive pricing, early access |

**Campaigns (Manual):**
- Weekly product spotlight (Tues, 10 AM CST)
- Monthly "Build of the Month" customer feature
- Holiday sales: Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Black Friday
- New arrival announcements for brand drops

**Year 1 Email KPIs:**
- List size target: 5,000 subscribers by Month 12
- Average open rate: 28%
- Average click rate: 3.5%
- Email-attributed revenue: 18% of total

---

### Channel 5: Influencer & Community Marketing
**Priority:** MEDIUM | **Budget:** $1,500/month

**Mechanic Influencers (Micro, 10K–100K followers):**
- Negotiate product-for-post deals (free parts + $150–400 per video)
- Target: automotive YouTube channels focused on DIY repair
- Require: honest reviews, fitment confirmation, link in description

**Forum Strategy (No-cost, high-trust):**
- Create verified brand accounts on: F150Forum, JEEPFORUM, ChevyTalk, DodgeTalk
- Participate genuinely in fitment threads
- Offer forum-exclusive discount codes ($10 off, tracked)

**Brand Ambassador Program (Month 6):**
- Recruit 5 regional "AllAmerican Mechanics"
- Provide parts at cost + revenue share on referred sales
- Content requirements: 2 posts/month featuring parts in use

---

### Channel 6: Partnerships
**Priority:** LOW-MEDIUM | **Timeline:** Year 2**

- **Speed shops & performance tuners** — cross-refer customers
- **Truck shows & Jeep events** — vendor booth presence
- **Trade associations** — SEMA membership, ASA (Automotive Service Association)
- **Roadside assistance apps** — referral for vehicle-specific breakdown parts

## 3. Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Month 1–3 (Launch Phase)
1. Launch Google Shopping with top 50 SKUs
2. Set up email capture popup (10% off first order)
3. Post 3x/week on Instagram + Facebook
4. Reach out to 10 micro-influencers for product seeding
5. Publish 8 cornerstone SEO articles
6. Enable cart abandonment flows on day one

### Month 4–6 (Growth Phase)
1. Increase PPC budget as ROAS is validated
2. Launch YouTube channel (2 install videos)
3. Start forum outreach program
4. Run first Memorial Day sale promotion
5. Launch referral program ($15 store credit per referral)

### Month 7–12 (Scale Phase)
1. Add Performance Max campaigns
2. Launch loyalty/rewards points program
3. Begin affiliate program for mechanic bloggers
4. 4th of July + Labor Day promotional campaigns
5. Prepare Black Friday / Cyber Monday strategy (biggest revenue week)

## 5. Retention Strategy

**Loyalty Program:**
- Earn 1 point per $1 spent
- 100 points = $5 store credit
- Bonus multipliers on premium brands

**Review Strategy:**
- Post-purchase email at 14 days: "How's the part holding up?"
- $5 store credit for verified review
- Target: 4.7+ average rating public-facing

**Customer Service as Marketing:**
- Every resolved complaint gets a 10% off next order coupon
- "Fitment Guarantee" banner on every product page — reinforces trust
