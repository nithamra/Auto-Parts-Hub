# AllAmerican Auto Parts — Business Plan
**Confidential | July 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

AllAmerican Auto Parts is an e-commerce retailer specializing in OEM and high-performance aftermarket parts for America's most iconic vehicles — Ford, Chevrolet, GMC, Jeep, and Dodge. We sell directly to consumers and light commercial shops through a dark-themed, high-trust digital storefront built for the working mechanic and performance enthusiast.

Our competitive advantage is a curated, brand-authenticated catalog paired with a proprietary Vehicle Finder tool that eliminates fitment guesswork, dramatically reducing returns and increasing customer confidence.

**Business Type:** E-commerce (B2C + B2B light)
**Launch Stage:** MVP online store with full catalog
**Target Year 1 Revenue:** $480,000
**Gross Margin Target:** 38–42%

---

## 1. Company Overview

### Mission
To be the most trusted online source for American-vehicle parts — delivering OEM quality, aftermarket performance, and expert fitment guidance with the speed and grit that American drivers expect.

### Vision
A nationally recognized brand serving every truck owner, weekend warrior, and shop mechanic who drives or repairs American iron.

### Core Values
- **Built Right** — We sell parts that work, period.
- **No Guesswork** — Vehicle Finder + expert specs on every listing.
- **Speed** — Orders ship same-day for in-stock items.
- **Trust** — Manufacturer warranties honored, no-hassle returns.

## 3. Market Analysis

### Target Market

**Primary: DIY Mechanics (35%)**
- Age 25–55, male-skewing (78%)
- Own 1–3 vehicles, at least one American truck or Jeep
- Purchase 4–8 parts/year, avg. $175 per order
- Research online, price-sensitive but brand-aware

**Secondary: Performance Enthusiasts (30%)**
- Age 22–45
- Willingness to pay premium for brand names
- Avg. order value $420
- High repeat purchase rate (2.8x/year)

**Tertiary: Small Independent Shops (25%)**
- Purchase in volume, want fast fulfillment
- Price-sensitive on commodity parts, loyal once trust is established
- Avg. order $850, 6–10 orders/year

**Quaternary: Fleet Owners (10%)**
- Government, construction, landscaping
- Long-term contracts, net-30 billing

### Market Size
- US auto parts aftermarket (2025): **$87.9B** and growing at 4.1% CAGR
- E-commerce share of auto parts: **28%** (growing 9% YoY)
- Addressable online market for American-brand specialty parts: **~$6.2B**

### Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Strength | Weakness |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| AutoZone.com | Brand recognition | Poor fitment tools, generic |
| RockAuto | Huge catalog, low prices | Confusing UX, no community |
| Summit Racing | Performance focus | Expensive, complex navigation |
| AmericanMuscle | Ford Mustang niche | Narrow vehicle coverage |
| **AllAmerican Auto** | Vehicle Finder, curated catalog, brand trust | New entrant, building SEO |

## 4. Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Product Sales** (Primary) — Direct e-commerce margin
2. **Rush Shipping Upsell** — $12.99 overnight option, ~22% uptake
3. **Extended Warranty Add-on** — $8–25 per order, ~15% uptake
4. **B2B Wholesale Portal** — Net-30 accounts for verified shops (Year 2)
5. **Affiliate / Referral Program** — Mechanic influencers and forums (Year 2)

### Cost Structure
| Category | % of Revenue |
|---------|-------------|
| Cost of Goods Sold | 60% |
| Fulfillment & Shipping | 8% |
| Marketing & Advertising | 12% |
| Technology & Hosting | 2% |
| Staff & Operations | 10% |
| Admin & Overhead | 5% |
| **Net Margin (Target)** | **~3–8%** |

### Pricing Strategy
- **MSRP + 5%** on premium/performance parts (demand inelastic)
- **Match + $2 under** key SKUs vs. AutoZone and RockAuto on commodity items
- **Bundle pricing** — Oil change kits, brake service kits (boosts AOV 22%)
- **Loyalty pricing** — 5% off for registered accounts after 2nd order

## 5. Operations

### Fulfillment Model (Year 1)
- **Dropship** from distributors (LKQ, Worldpac, Parts Authority) for long-tail SKUs
- **In-house stock** for top 200 fast-moving SKUs in a leased 2,000 sq ft facility
- Target: 85% of orders ship same day, 99% within 48 hours

### Technology Stack
- Storefront: React + Vite (custom build, full-stack)
- Backend API: Express + PostgreSQL
- Payments: Stripe
- Email: Postmark (transactional) + Klaviyo (marketing)
- Analytics: Mixpanel + Google Analytics 4
- Reviews: Judge.me

### Customer Service
- Live chat (business hours, 8 AM–6 PM CST)
- Email support (24-hour SLA)
- Phone (Year 2)
- Self-service returns portal
