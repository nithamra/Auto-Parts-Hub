American Auto Parts Store – Full-Stack Web Application

Build a fully responsive, modern e-commerce platform for an American auto parts retailer. The app targets both DIY customers and B2B clients (workshops, fleets, dealers) with a professional, dark-themed UI accented in red and blue.

Core User Features

Home: Hero with truck/SUV imagery, brand showcase (Ford, Chevrolet, GMC, Jeep), featured products, promotions, testimonials, and CTAs.

Catalog: Products organized by brand & category (engine, brakes, suspension, etc.). Each product includes images, SKU, compatibility, price, availability, specs, manufacturer, warranty, ratings & reviews. Advanced filtering (brand, category, price), search, sorting, and pagination.

Product Detail: Image gallery with zoom, specs, compatibility, reviews (rate/write, upload photos, like), Q&A, add-to-cart/quote/wishlist.

Vehicle Compatibility Finder: Search by brand, year, model, engine, trim to show only compatible parts.

Cart & Checkout: Add/remove items, update quantities, apply coupons, estimate shipping; collect customer/shipping/billing info, delivery/payment methods; order confirmation page.

Quote Request: Multi-product quotes with VIN entry, part description, photo upload; receive confirmation.

Bulk Orders: Dedicated form for businesses (workshops, dealers, fleets) requesting large quantities – includes business info, product list, quantities, delivery notes.

Wishlist & Order Tracking: Save items; track orders by number and view history.

User Accounts: Registration, login, password reset, profile management, saved addresses, wishlist, order history, saved vehicles.

Contact & FAQ: Form, phone, email, WhatsApp, map, hours; FAQ covering shipping, returns, warranty, compatibility, payments, installation.

Admin Dashboard
Manage products (CRUD, inventory, prices, images), categories, brands, orders, quotes, bulk orders, reviews, customer accounts; view analytics, sales reports, low-stock alerts.

Design & UX

Fully responsive (desktop/tablet/mobile)
